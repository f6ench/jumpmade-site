Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 March 2025

Who we are

TradeAhead is a trading name of Jumpmade. We provide automated lead response systems for UK tradespeople. Our website is tradeahead.co.uk.

What data we collect

When you use our website or services, we may collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number (via booking forms and newsletter signup)
  • Business name and trade type
  • Quiz responses (trade, lead volume, job value, response time)
  • Usage data via cookies (see below)

How we use your data

  • To provide and manage the services you've signed up for
  • To send you the newsletter you subscribed to
  • To contact you about your booking or enquiry
  • To improve our website and services

Cookies

We use cookies from third-party services including GoHighLevel (booking and CRM) and Trustpilot (reviews). These cookies help us deliver and measure our services. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. We share data only with service providers who help us deliver our services (e.g., GoHighLevel for CRM, email, and SMS).

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as you are a client or subscriber. You can request deletion at any time.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the details below.
